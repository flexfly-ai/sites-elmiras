Our Story

A Second Home for Your Family

Since 2002, Elmira's Daycare has been a second home for children and families in San Diego. We provide a warm, nurturing environment where children ages 12 months to 4 years can learn, play, and grow.

Our bilingual English/Russian program offers children the gift of multilingual development while fostering creativity through play-based learning, music, and art.

We believe in the power of home-cooked meals, genuine care, and creating lasting friendships that extend beyond our walls.
